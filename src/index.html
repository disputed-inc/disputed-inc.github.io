Stop Insulting your Customers

Building your brand means treating your customers with respect. When you fight 100% of your chargebacks, you're insulting customers who truly were affected by fraudulent use of their card or who truly are in the right about their dispute. You may win more chargebacks initially, but they'll usually be re-opened and you'll have alienated your customers and damaged your brand.

For your business it may make sense to fight everything or it may not. With Disputed, you get the power to choose. With tools to better defend your bottom line without alienating your customers, Disputed takes care of you, so you can take care of your brand and grow your revenue.
